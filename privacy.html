We also collect non-personal information, such as browser type, operating system, and web pages visited to help us manage our website.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services.
  • Personalize our services and provide advertisements, content, or features that match user profiles or interests.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with laws or to respond to lawful requests and legal process.
